DICE GAME

INSTRUCTIONS

Rolling Dice
Each Player has their own button. Click the button to roll the dice

Each Player will have 3 turns to roll the dice.

Please Do not hit the button more than 3 turns.

LEVELS

Levels will change after every three turns.

Level 1 - 4-Sided Dice

Level 2 - 6-Sided Dice

Level 3 - 8-Sided Dice

Level 4 - 10-Sided Dice

Level 5 - 12-Sided Dice

Level 6 - 20-Sided Dice

SCORING

Both Players start at Level 1

The Player whose score is more after 3 turns will be jumped to Next Level and will be able to roll that level Dice.

BONUS POINTS- If any Player scores max in a single turn for that Level will be awarded 5 Bonus Points(BP).

First Player to score 100 WINS THE GAME !
